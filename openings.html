Hengyun Neil Li - Openings (Year-round Recruitment)

Dr. Hengyun Li is currently an Associate Professor with tenure in the School of Hotel and Tourism Management (SHTM) at the Hong Kong Polytechnic University (PolyU).

Our research group are actively recruiting self-motivated Postdoc, Ph.D. students, Dual PhD Degree students, MPhil/Msc, and Research Assistants, etc. Visiting scholars, interns, and self-funded students are also welcome. Send me an email if you are interested.

>>> Research areas: Applied econometrics, Machine/Deep learning, and Experimental design etc.

>>> Accepted research majors: Finance, Economics, Econometrics, Computer Science, Artificial Intelligence, Statistics, Mathematics, and Hospitality and Tourism Management etc.

Please visit the Fellowship & Scholarship Schemes (Graduate School) website for further details. [Link]

[Admission Requirements]

  • Hong Kong PhD Fellowship Scheme(HKPFS)，monthly stipend: HK$28,100

  • PolyU Presidential PhD Fellowship Scheme (PPPFS)，monthly stipend: HK$27,790

  • PolyU Research Postgraduate Scholarship (PRPgS), monthly stipend: HK$18,840

  • Dual PhD Degree Programmes, monthly stipend: HK$18,840

  • Several Research Assistant (RA) positions are available now!

Drop me an email if you are interesting.

Application Process:

If you are interested in a position, please feel free to contact Hengyun at: neilhengyun.li(at)polyu(dot)edu(dot)hk.

Below are some suggestions when contacting via email:

Please…

  1. Use a subject line as follows: {$position} Open Position {$YourName}， where {$position} should be ‘‘Postdoc’’, ‘‘PhD’’, ‘‘MPhil’’, ‘‘MSs’’, ‘‘BS’’, ‘‘Visiting’’, or ‘‘RA’’.
    e.g., If I were applying for a Research Assistant position I would use the subject ‘‘RA Open Position Hengyun Li’’

  2. Provide a few sentences to introduce yourself and express your interest in the position

  3. Attach your CV/Resume Including the following (if applicable): GPA/ranking, TOEFL/IELTS/GRE scores, and publications/research experience, awards, etc.

Thank you and I look forward to hearing from you!
